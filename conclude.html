Conclusion

To summarize all of this information up, eXtended Reality combines all of the features of existing virtual simulation technologies into one single technology and offers those features through the use of a spectrum to determine the level of virtuallity. Even though the technology is at its early stage, soon more companies and businesses will adopt the technology for use with training sessions, simulations, and other types of virtual simulation that the three types of virtual technology can offer.
